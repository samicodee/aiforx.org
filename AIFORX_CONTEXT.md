# AIFORX Context

## Core Positioning

AIFORX is an applied AI education platform for non-technical professionals who want to use AI in real work, not study AI theory.

The focus is on working professionals in India and the Middle East who need practical AI fluency for operations, productivity, decision-making, automation, and business transformation.

The first flagship offer is **AI for Operators**, a premium Hyderabad-first in-person workshop for founder-operators who run real businesses and want to use AI for practical execution.

## Founder-Led Angle

AIFORX is led by Sami, a Hyderabad-based founder and operator with 10 years of experience across operations, sales, logistics, hospitality, the popcorn industry, and business building.

Sami built Country Chicken Co., India's first naturally raised chicken brand, and has operated multi-crore businesses. The positioning should make this clear: AIFORX is not taught like a generic AI class. It is led by an operator who has built and run real businesses.

## Primary Audience

- Founders with heavy operations responsibilities
- Founder-operators running SME businesses between roughly Rs 1Cr and Rs 25Cr in annual revenue
- Doctors and healthcare professionals
- Operators, CEOs, CXOs, managers, and business leaders
- Non-technical engineers, including civil, mechanical, and electrical engineers
- Corporate teams and departments
- Working-class professionals who want to upskill with AI

## Geographic Focus

- India
- Middle East

## Audience Traits

- Mostly non-technical
- Time-constrained and career-oriented
- Interested in practical ROI from AI
- Often responsible for people, processes, clients, operations, or domain workflows
- Needs confidence using AI tools without becoming a software engineer
- Values examples from real business, professional, and operational contexts

## Differentiation Direction

AIFORX should avoid sounding like a generic AI tools workshop. The stronger positioning is:

- Applied AI for real operators
- AI fluency for professionals who run teams, processes, and businesses
- Practical workflows over theory
- Domain-specific AI use cases over generic prompt lists
- India and Middle East context over US-centric examples
- Human judgment, process design, and execution over hype

## Competitive Context

Comparable programs include Outskill, GrowthSchool, Future Agents, ONROL, atomcamp, SureSkill, Maven AI cohorts, Section AI, and DeepLearning.AI short courses.

AIFORX should compete by being more practical, more role-specific, and more credible for non-technical professionals than broad AI masterclasses.

## Cohort 1 Defaults

- Offer name: AI for Operators
- Location: Hyderabad
- Format: 2-day in-person workshop, Saturday and Sunday, 8-9 hours per day
- Price: Rs 60,000, full pay only
- Cohort size: 25-35 participants
- Target participant: founder-operator or business decision-maker running a Rs 1Cr-25Cr business
- Support: 90 days through WhatsApp and 6 biweekly implementation clinics
- Delivery language: English + Hinglish
- Delivery team: Sami + 2 assistants
- Core output: 3 working AI workflows for ops, sales, and hiring/team execution

## Content Principles

- Teach by workflows, not tool lists
- Use real professional scenarios
- Keep language simple and business-first
- Avoid overpromising career transformation
- Show before/after examples of work improved by AI
- Include templates, playbooks, checklists, and live builds
- Make each module produce an immediately usable output

## Brand Tone

Practical, direct, credible, modern, and regionally relevant.

Avoid excessive hype, vague transformation claims, and purely technical jargon.
