# AI for Operators: 2-Day Workshop Agenda

## Pre-Work

Send 5-7 days before the workshop.

- Application intake completed
- Participant submits one ops problem, one sales problem, and one hiring/team problem
- Participant confirms laptop, Google account, and AI tool access
- Participant shares business type, team size, revenue band, and current operating bottlenecks

## Day 1: AI for Real Business Execution

### 9:30-10:15 - Operator-Led Opening

- Sami's founder/operator story
- Why generic AI learning fails operators
- Where AI helps in real businesses: thinking, drafting, reviewing, tracking, delegating

### 10:15-11:30 - AI Basics for Operators

- What LLMs are, only at the practical level
- What AI is good and bad at
- Human judgment and review loops
- Common failure modes: hallucination, vague prompts, bad data, no owner

### 11:45-1:15 - Prompting for Operators

Framework:

- Context
- Role
- Task
- Inputs/data
- Output format
- Review criteria

Output: participant creates a reusable prompt format for their business.

### 2:15-4:00 - Build 1: Ops Workflow

Use cases:

- SOP creation
- Daily report summarization
- Vendor/client issue handling
- MIS summary from raw updates

Output: one working ops workflow in Docs/Sheets.

### 4:15-6:00 - Build 2: Sales Workflow

Use cases:

- Lead qualification
- Follow-up drafts
- Proposal improvement
- Objection handling
- Sales tracking in Sheets

Output: one working sales follow-up and tracking workflow.

## Day 2: Team, Hiring, Templates, Implementation

### 9:30-10:30 - Day 1 Review

- Participant demos
- Common corrections
- Upgrade prompts into reusable workflows

### 10:30-12:30 - Build 3: Hiring And Team Workflow

Use cases:

- JD drafting
- Candidate screening
- Interview scorecards
- Onboarding checklist
- Delegation prompts for managers

Output: one working hiring/team workflow.

### 1:30-3:00 - Convert Workflows Into Operating Templates

- Turn prompts into checklists
- Build trackers in Google Sheets
- Create reusable folders and naming conventions
- Define who owns each workflow in the business

### 3:15-4:30 - 90-Day Implementation Plan

- Pick 3 workflows to implement first
- Assign owner, cadence, expected output, and review ritual
- Define what success looks like in 30, 60, and 90 days

### 4:30-5:30 - Support Onboarding

- WhatsApp group rules
- Clinic schedule
- How to ask for help
- What assistants can help with
- What Sami will review during clinics

### 5:30-6:00 - Certificate And Close

- Certificate issued
- Participant commitment capture
- Testimonials and referral request
