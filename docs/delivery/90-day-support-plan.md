# AI for Operators: 90-Day Support Plan

## Support Promise

Participants get structured implementation support for 90 days after the workshop through:

- WhatsApp community
- 6 biweekly implementation clinics
- Template and workflow support from assistants
- Operator-level review from Sami during clinics

## WhatsApp Group Rules

- Ask specific workflow questions, not broad AI questions
- Share screenshots or examples when asking for help
- No spam, promotions, or unrelated forwards
- Respect confidentiality of business data
- Remove sensitive customer, employee, or financial details before sharing

## Clinic Cadence

Clinic 1: Week 2  
Theme: Fix and deploy the first ops workflow

Clinic 2: Week 4  
Theme: Sales follow-up and tracking workflow review

Clinic 3: Week 6  
Theme: Hiring/team workflow implementation

Clinic 4: Week 8  
Theme: Reporting, MIS, and owner review systems

Clinic 5: Week 10  
Theme: Workflow adoption by team members

Clinic 6: Week 12  
Theme: Results review, next workflows, referrals, testimonials

## Assistant Role

Assistants help with:

- Prompt formatting
- Sheet structure
- Docs and Forms setup
- Template cleanup
- Basic troubleshooting

Assistants do not provide done-for-you consulting, custom software development, or unlimited private implementation.

## Sami's Role

Sami leads:

- Operator judgment
- Workflow prioritization
- Business use-case review
- Implementation corrections
- Founder-level decision making

## Success Metrics

By day 90, each participant should have:

- At least 3 workflows actively used in the business
- At least 1 team member using or maintaining a workflow
- A basic review cadence for AI-assisted outputs
- A clear next list of 3-5 workflows to build
