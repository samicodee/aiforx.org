# AI for Operators: Cohort 1 Offer Brief

## Positioning

AI for Operators is a premium 2-day in-person workshop for founder-operators running Rs 1Cr-25Cr businesses in Hyderabad.

It is not positioned as a generic AI tools workshop. It is an operator-led implementation room where participants build practical AI workflows for ops, sales, hiring, reporting, and daily execution.

## Founder Story

AIFORX is led by Sami, a Hyderabad-based founder and operator with 10 years of experience across operations, logistics, hospitality, the popcorn industry, sales, and business building.

Sami built Country Chicken Co., India's first naturally raised chicken brand, and has operated multi-crore businesses. The core trust hook is:

> Not taught by an AI teacher. Led by an operator who has built and run real businesses.

## Commercials

- Price: Rs 60,000
- Payment: full pay only after application acceptance
- Cohort size: 25-35 participants
- Location: Hyderabad
- Format: Saturday-Sunday, 8-9 hours per day
- Support: 90 days after the workshop
- Delivery: Sami + 2 assistants
- Language: English + Hinglish

## Ideal Participant

- Founder, co-founder, owner, operator, or business decision-maker
- Runs or leads a Rs 1Cr-25Cr business
- Owns outcomes in ops, sales, hiring, reporting, or team execution
- Wants practical AI adoption without becoming technical
- Can attend both full days and commit to the 90-day implementation process

## Core Promise

Participants leave with 3 working AI workflows:

1. Ops workflow: SOPs, daily reports, issue handling, MIS summaries, operating reviews
2. Sales workflow: lead qualification, follow-up, proposals, objections, CRM-style tracking
3. Hiring/team workflow: JDs, screening, scorecards, onboarding, delegation

## What This Does Not Promise

- No coding bootcamp
- No fully deployed app guarantee
- No AI engineering curriculum
- No generic list of 100 AI tools
- No claim that AI will replace the participant's judgment or team

## Tools

- ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini
- Google Sheets, Docs, Forms
- Optional demonstrations of lightweight automation
- Avoid making n8n, Make, Airtable, or app deployment mandatory for cohort 1
