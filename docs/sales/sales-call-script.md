# AI for Operators: Sales Call Script

## Call Goal

Qualify whether the applicant is a serious founder-operator with real business pain, decision authority, ability to pay Rs 60,000, and willingness to implement.

## Opening

Thanks for applying for AI for Operators. This is not a generic AI tools class. It is a 2-day implementation workshop for operators who want to build practical AI workflows for their business. I want to understand your business first and see if this cohort is the right fit.

## Qualification Questions

1. What business do you run or operate?
2. What is your role in daily decision-making?
3. What does your current team look like?
4. Where are you losing the most time operationally?
5. What sales, follow-up, or customer communication process is currently weak?
6. What hiring, team, or delegation problem keeps repeating?
7. Have you used ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI tool before?
8. If you had 3 AI-assisted workflows running in your business 90 days from now, what would you want them to be?
9. Can you attend the full Saturday-Sunday workshop in Hyderabad?
10. Are you ready to pay Rs 60,000 in full if accepted?

## Positioning Explanation

This is led by Sami, a founder/operator from Hyderabad with 10 years across operations, logistics, hospitality, sales, and business building. The workshop is built from an operator's lens, not a teacher's lens.

The promise is practical: you will build 3 working workflows for ops, sales, and hiring/team execution. We are not promising coding, custom software, or a fully deployed app. We will use tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Google Sheets, Docs, and Forms.

## Close

Based on what you shared, I think you are a fit for cohort 1 because you have real operating problems and you can implement after the workshop.

The fee is Rs 60,000 full pay. Your seat is confirmed only after payment. After the 2-day workshop, you will also get 90 days of support through WhatsApp and 6 biweekly implementation clinics.

## Objection Handling

### I already use ChatGPT.

Good. This workshop is not about opening ChatGPT for the first time. It is about turning AI into repeatable workflows for ops, sales, hiring, reporting, and delegation.

### Is this technical?

No. It is built for non-technical operators. We will use AI tools, Google Sheets, Docs, and Forms. No coding is required.

### Will I get a deployed app?

No. This cohort is not a software-building bootcamp. You will leave with working operating workflows and templates. Deployed apps can become a later advanced track or separate implementation engagement.

### Why Rs 60,000?

Because this is a premium implementation room, not a mass workshop. It includes 2 full in-person days, hands-on build support, templates, a curated founder-operator room, and 90 days of follow-up implementation support.
