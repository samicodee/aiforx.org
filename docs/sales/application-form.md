# AI for Operators: Application Form

## Form Questions

1. Full name
2. Phone / WhatsApp number
3. Email
4. City
5. Business name
6. Website or Instagram/LinkedIn page
7. Your role in the business
8. Industry
9. Approx annual revenue
   - Below Rs 1Cr
   - Rs 1Cr-5Cr
   - Rs 5Cr-25Cr
   - Above Rs 25Cr
10. Team size
11. What are the top 3 operational problems you want AI to help with?
12. What is one sales or customer follow-up problem you want to fix?
13. What is one hiring, team, or delegation problem you want to fix?
14. What AI tools have you already used?
15. Can you attend both full days in Hyderabad?
16. Are you comfortable with the Rs 60,000 full-pay fee if accepted?
17. Why do you want to join this cohort now?

## Scoring

Score each applicant out of 20.

- Decision authority: 0-5
- Business pain clarity: 0-5
- Ability to pay: 0-4
- Implementation intent: 0-4
- Room fit: 0-2

## Acceptance Guidance

- 16-20: accept if seat is available
- 12-15: accept only if the call confirms strong intent
- Below 12: do not accept for cohort 1

## Reject Or Defer If

- The person wants only general AI awareness
- They are not responsible for business outcomes
- They cannot attend both full days
- They expect custom software or done-for-you implementation inside the 2-day workshop
- They are primarily looking for a job certificate
