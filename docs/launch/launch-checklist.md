# AI for Operators: Launch Checklist

## 4-6 Weeks Before

- Confirm workshop date and venue
- Confirm AV, Wi-Fi, seating, lunch, tea, parking, and power outlets
- Finalize landing page
- Finalize application form
- Build target invite list from founder network and referrals
- Prepare paid ad creative that drives to application
- Create WhatsApp follow-up message templates
- Create payment confirmation process

## 2-3 Weeks Before

- Run application calls
- Confirm accepted participants after full payment
- Send pre-work form
- Send laptop and account setup instructions
- Prepare participant spreadsheet with business type, pain points, and workflow needs
- Confirm assistants and delivery responsibilities
- Prepare certificates

## 1 Week Before

- Finalize room layout
- Print participant list and check-in sheet
- Prepare slide deck
- Prepare Docs/Sheets/Form templates
- Send reminder with date, venue, timing, and what to bring
- Create WhatsApp group but add participants after payment confirmation

## Workshop Days

- Check-in and seating
- Verify AI tool and Google account access
- Capture before/after workflow examples
- Capture testimonials at the end of Day 2
- Confirm each participant's 90-day implementation plan

## After Workshop

- Send certificate
- Share template folder
- Share clinic calendar
- Start WhatsApp support rhythm
- Track participant implementation progress
- Ask for referrals after Clinic 2 and testimonials after Clinic 6
